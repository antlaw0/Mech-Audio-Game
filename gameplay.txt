##Controls
All controls should be remappable so player can adjust key bindings to their liking. I would like this to potentially be playable on mobile phones with on-screen controls, maybe controller support, just keep controls flexible.
Here is a list of game actions and initial key bindings. I may add more actions or modify at a later time:
left arrow:  turn left
right arrow: turn right
arrow up:  move forward
arrow backward:  move backwards
W:  aim up
S:  aim down
F:  right arm weapon/item
A:  left arm weapon/item
R:  right shoulder weapon/item
Q:  left shoulder weapon/item
Spacebarr: jump/fly
Tab:  pause menu


##Mech stats

All the stats a mech has. Building a mech with different parts affects these status. These stats should be for both player mechs as well as enemy/npc mechs.

HP:  hit points
energy defense:  damage reduction from energy based attacks
physical defense:  damage reduction from physical attacks
Max energy capacity:  The amount of energy your mech  can hold
energy regeneration:  how quickly your mech regenerates energy

ground turn speed:  how fast your mech rotates left/right while on ground
Weight:  how much does your mech weigh. Weight effects a variety of things such as movement and turn speed, energy drain while flying, knockback from heavy impacts, ect. Also, your locomotion part has a max weight that it can carry.
top ground speed:  the maximum speed your mech can move on ground
ground acceleration: how quickly it takes for your mech to reach top speed on ground
top flight speed:  top speed your mech can fly
flight turn speed:  how fast your mech turns left/right while flying
flight acceleration:  how quickly your mech can reach its top flight speed
Flight energy drain:  how much does staying airborne cost to energy. The heavier your mech is, the more drain there is as well.
